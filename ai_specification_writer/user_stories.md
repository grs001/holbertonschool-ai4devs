# User Stories - Carpool Connect

### User Story 1
As a passenger, I want to search for nearby drivers so that I can find a quick ride.

**Acceptance Criteria**:
- User enters pickup and destination.
- System displays available drivers within 5 km.
- Ride can be booked in under 2 minutes.

**Priority**: MVP

---

### User Story 2
As a driver, I want to post my available route so that passengers can find and book my ride.

**Acceptance Criteria**:
- Driver enters start point, destination, date, time, and available seats.
- Ride is visible to passengers within the route range immediately after posting.
- Driver receives a notification when a passenger books a seat.

**Priority**: MVP

---

### User Story 3
As a passenger, I want to pay for my ride through the app so that I do not need to carry cash.

**Acceptance Criteria**:
- User can add a credit/debit card or digital wallet.
- Payment is processed automatically upon ride confirmation.
- User receives a payment receipt via email or in-app notification.

**Priority**: MVP

---

### User Story 4
As a driver, I want to rate passengers after a trip so that I can maintain a trustworthy community.

**Acceptance Criteria**:
- Driver is prompted to rate the passenger after trip completion.
- Rating is on a 1–5 star scale with an optional comment.
- Rating is reflected on the passenger's profile within 24 hours.

**Priority**: High

---

### User Story 5
As a passenger, I want to rate and review drivers so that other users can make informed decisions.

**Acceptance Criteria**:
- Passenger is prompted to rate the driver after trip completion.
- Rating includes stars and an optional written review.
- Reviews are publicly visible on the driver's profile.

**Priority**: High

---

### User Story 6
As a passenger, I want to see my eco-impact after each trip so that I feel motivated to carpool.

**Acceptance Criteria**:
- App displays estimated CO2 saved compared to a solo car trip.
- Cumulative eco-impact is shown on the user's profile dashboard.
- A monthly eco-report can be downloaded or shared.

**Priority**: Medium

---

### User Story 7
As a passenger, I want to share my trip details with a contact so that someone knows my location for safety.

**Acceptance Criteria**:
- User can select a contact to share live trip details via SMS or app link.
- Shared link shows real-time location of the vehicle.
- Sharing stops automatically when the trip ends.

**Priority**: High

---

### User Story 8
As a driver, I want to schedule recurring rides for my daily commute so that I do not have to re-enter details every day.

**Acceptance Criteria**:
- Driver can set a ride to repeat on selected weekdays.
- Recurring rides are automatically posted at the scheduled time.
- Driver can pause or cancel the recurring schedule at any time.

**Priority**: Medium

---

### User Story 9
As a passenger, I want to filter available rides by departure time and gender preference so that I feel comfortable and safe.

**Acceptance Criteria**:
- Filter options include time range and gender preference.
- Results update in real time as filters are applied.
- Selected filters are saved for future searches.

**Priority**: Medium

---

### User Story 10
As a user, I want to chat with my matched driver or passenger in the app so that I can coordinate pickup details.

**Acceptance Criteria**:
- In-app chat is enabled once a ride is confirmed.
- Messages are delivered in real time.
- Chat history is available until 24 hours after trip completion.

**Priority**: High

---

### User Story 11
As a passenger, I want to receive a notification when my booked driver is nearby so that I am ready on time.

**Acceptance Criteria**:
- Push notification is sent when the driver is within 1 km of the pickup point.
- Notification includes driver name, vehicle description, and ETA.
- User can track the driver's live location on the map.

**Priority**: MVP

---

### User Story 12
As an administrator, I want to view reported users and take action so that the platform remains safe.

**Acceptance Criteria**:
- Admin dashboard displays all user reports with details and status.
- Admin can warn, suspend, or ban a user from the dashboard.
- Affected user receives an automated email notification of the action taken.

**Priority**: Low
