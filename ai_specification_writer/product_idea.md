# Product Idea

## Vision
Enable commuters to share rides efficiently, reducing travel costs and carbon emissions through intelligent real-time matching.

## Users

- **Drivers**: want to fill empty seats, share fuel costs, and reduce their daily commute expenses.
- **Passengers**: want affordable, reliable, and safe rides without owning a vehicle.
- **Administrators**: want to monitor platform activity, resolve disputes, and ensure user safety.

## Key Features

- Real-time ride matching based on route, timing, and preferences
- Payment integration with multiple options (card, wallet, cash)
- Ratings and reviews system for drivers and passengers
- Eco-impact tracking showing CO2 savings per trip
- In-app chat for driver-passenger communication
- Schedule recurring rides for daily commuters
- Safety features including trip sharing and SOS button
